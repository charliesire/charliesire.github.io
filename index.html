Charlie SIRE   


I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Inria Paris Saclay and Ecole Polytechnique (CMAP), under the supervision of Josselin Garnier. My Ph.D thesis was defended at IRSN and was supervised by Rodolphe LE RICHE and Didier RULLIERE. Here is my CV.

Research interests

I study uncertainty quantification in the context of expensive-to-evaluate simulators. Gaussian Process Regression is at the core of my work, addressing various questions: Inversion, Optimization, Quantization, Design of Experiments, Bayesian Calibration.
